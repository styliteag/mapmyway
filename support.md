# MapMyWay Support

[Privacy Policy](./) · Contact: **wb@stylite.de**

MapMyWay is a privacy-first iPhone location tracker. It records your day in the
background and shows it as a timeline and on a map. Everything stays on your
device; optional exports and backups go to your own private iCloud Drive.

## Get help

Email **wb@stylite.de** with your question. Include your iOS version and a short
description of what you expected versus what happened. There is no account, so
we never ask for a password.

## Common questions

**Nothing is being recorded.**
Open **Settings → Tracking** and make sure Automatic Tracking is on. In the iOS
Settings app, set MapMyWay's location permission to **Always** — without it, the
app can only record while it is open in the foreground. Motion access must also
be allowed so trips can be classified.

**The timeline is empty.**
A fresh install has no history yet. Walk around for a few minutes with the app
installed, or open **Settings → Load demo data** to populate a sample week and
see how the app looks with data.

**Where is my data?**
On your iPhone, in the app's private database. If iCloud is enabled, daily GPX
exports and weekly database backups are written to
`iCloud Drive/MapMyWay/`. Only you can read them.

**How do I export or delete my data?**
GPX and CSV files are written automatically to iCloud Drive in open formats —
move them anywhere. To delete everything, remove the app (the on-device
database goes with it) and delete the `MapMyWay` folder in the Files app.
